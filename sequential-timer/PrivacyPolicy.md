# Privacy Policy

**Document version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 7/23/2026
**Last updated:** 7/23/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sequential Timer (“the App”) handles information.

## Summary

The App is designed to store timer definitions and evaluation results locally on the user’s device. The current version does not require an account and does not transmit timer or result data to the developer.

## Information Stored on the Device

The App may store:

- timer names, event names, and timer configurations;
- candidate or participant names entered by the user;
- event times, dates, completion status, and result history;
- imported `.sqconfig` files and configurations created from them; and
- app preferences and diagnostic information supplied by the operating system.

This information remains on the device unless the user chooses to export, copy, email, text, share, back up, or otherwise transmit it.

## Sharing and Third-Party Apps

When the user invokes email, text messaging, file import, file sharing, clipboard, backup, or another system service, the operating system or a selected third-party app processes that information under its own terms and privacy policy. The developer does not control those services.

## Permissions

The App may request access only when needed for a user-initiated feature, such as selecting an import file or opening a share destination. Available permissions and behavior vary by device and operating system.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The App uses no developer-operated analytics, advertising SDK, cross-app tracking, or remote account service.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Local data remains until the user deletes it in the App, clears the App’s storage, or uninstalls the App. Shared copies, backups, messages, and files may remain elsewhere and must be deleted through the relevant service.

## Security

No storage or transmission method is completely secure. Users should avoid entering sensitive personal, medical, employment, or legally protected information unless their organization has approved the App and the device is appropriately secured.

## Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law and is not intended to collect children’s personal information.

## International Users

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of the App, including evaluation records and participant names, complies with applicable privacy, employment, education, records-retention, and data-transfer laws.

## Changes

The policy version and date will be updated when this policy changes. Material changes should be communicated through the App, its store listing, or another appropriate channel.
